About AZoom

Pioneering sustainable mobility in Singapore with affordable, premium electric vehicle rentals.

Our Mission

AZoom was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to make electric vehicle rental accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone in Singapore.

We believe that sustainable transportation is key to a greener future. By offering a curated fleet the latest EVs, primarily from innovative Chinese brands like BYD, XPENG, ZEEKR and more! We empower drivers to reduce their carbon footprint without compromising on performance or style.

Why Choose AZoom?

Unlike traditional car rental companies, we specialize exclusively in eco-friendly vehicles. Our EVs are up to 30% cheaper to rent than gasoline cars, thanks to lower maintenance and energy costs.

With two convenient locations across Singapore and partnerships with 12+ charging stations island-wide, we ensure a seamless experience from booking to return.
